## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Username (optional, can be pseudonymous)
- Password (hashed and encrypted, never stored in plain text)

**Course Progress:**
- Lesson completion status
- Quiz scores and attempts
- XP and badges earned
- Bookmarks and notes

**Q&A Service (Lightning Payments):**
- Questions submitted via Q&A form
- Lightning invoice payment confirmations (we do NOT store your Lightning wallet details or payment information—payments are processed through BTCPay Server or third-party Lightning providers)

**Communications:**
- Messages sent through contact forms
- Emails you send to us
- Feedback and survey responses

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited, time spent on pages
- Browser type, operating system
- Device information (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Referring website (how you found us)
- Date and time of visits

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:**
- Session cookies (remember login status)
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics or privacy-focused alternative like Plausible)
- Preference cookies (dark mode, language settings)

**We do NOT use:**
- Advertising trackers
- Social media pixels (Facebook Pixel, etc.)
- Invasive cross-site tracking

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We respect your privacy and do NOT collect:
- ❌ Your Bitcoin wallet addresses (unless you voluntarily share in Q&A questions)
- ❌ Your Bitcoin private keys (NEVER share these with anyone!)
- ❌ Your Bitcoin holdings or transaction history
- ❌ Sensitive personal information (SSN, government IDs, financial details)
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Precise geolocation data

**We will:**

If Soundsfair is acquired, merged, or sold, your information may be transferred to the new owner. We will:

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

### 4.1 Access and Download Your Data

You have the right to:
- Access all personal information we hold about you
- Download your data in a portable format (JSON or CSV)

**How to request:** Email us at privacy@soundsfair.com with subject "Data Access Request"

### 4.2 Correct or Update Your Information

You can:
- Update your email, username, or password in account settings
- Request corrections to inaccurate data

### 4.3 Delete Your Account

You have the right to:
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Be "forgotten" (GDPR right to erasure)

**How to delete:**
- Go to Account Settings → Delete Account
- Or email privacy@soundsfair.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"

**What happens:**
- Your account, email, course progress, and personal data are permanently deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics (no personal identifiers)
- Legal records (e.g., payment confirmations) may be retained as required by law

### 4.4 Opt Out of Communications

You can opt out of:
- **Marketing emails:** Click "unsubscribe" in any email
- **Newsletters:** Manage preferences in account settings
- **Cookies:** Use browser settings to block or delete cookies

**Note:** You cannot opt out of essential service emails (e.g., password reset, account verification)

### 4.5 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals:
- If your browser sends DNT=1, we disable non-essential analytics
- Essential functionality (login, progress tracking) still works

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 How We Protect Your Data

**Technical Measures:**
- **HTTPS/SSL encryption** for all data in transit
- **Password hashing** (bcrypt or Argon2, never plain text)
- **Database encryption** at rest
- **Regular security audits** and penetration testing
- **Rate limiting** to prevent brute-force attacks
- **Input validation** to prevent SQL injection, XSS attacks

**Organizational Measures:**
- **Access controls:** Only authorized personnel can access data
- **Principle of least privilege:** Staff have minimal access needed for their role
- **Security training:** Team trained on privacy and security best practices

### 5.2 Your Responsibility

**You must:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if available
- Never share your password
- Log out on shared devices
- Report suspected account compromise immediately

**Remember:**
- We will NEVER ask for your password via email or phone
- We will NEVER ask for your Bitcoin private keys

### 5.3 Data Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs affecting your personal information:
- We will notify you within 72 hours (as required by GDPR)
- We will explain what happened, what data was affected, and what we're doing about it
- We will provide guidance on protecting yourself (e.g., change password)

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

**Active accounts:**
- Retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete anytime

**Inactive accounts:**
- After 3 years of inactivity, we may email you to confirm you want to keep your account
- If no response within 90 days, account may be deleted

**Deleted accounts:**
- Personal data deleted within 30 days

- Legal/financial records retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

### 6.2 Backups

- Database backups retained for 30 days for disaster recovery
- Deleted account data purged from backups within 30-90 days
- Backups encrypted and securely stored

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

### 10.1 What Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies (Required):**
- Session cookies (remember you're logged in)

- Preference cookies (dark mode, language)

- ❌ Social media tracking pixels
- ❌ Cross-site tracking cookies

### 10.2 How to Manage Cookies

**Browser settings:**
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

**Opt-out:**
- Use our cookie consent banner (appears on first visit)
- Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser
- Use privacy extensions (uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger)

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may break functionality (you won't be able to log in).
